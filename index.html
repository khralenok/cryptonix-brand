Logo and mark

Our logo is word CRYPTONIX with all capital letters typed in custom font.

In all cases where full logo fits and reads well, it's usage is preferable. To define if there is enpugh space to place logo take in account it's safe area that is 1/2 of logo letter's height.

Examples of placements: website, social media graphics, documents and advertisement.

If you haven't space for full logo, you could use our brand mark, that is "C" letter of our name with specific proportions.

For Example: avatars, favicons, pins

We also use mark as a shape or pattern. You could learn more about it in shapes section of this guideline.

Colors

Our color palette builded on yellow and shades from black to white with our specific greenish tone. Accent color uses for highlighting elements that have highest level of importancy or in oposite could serve as background for promotion graphics

We also have alternative brand color - ◼ Orange(#FF5A00). You could use this color in combination with yellow as color accent on photo, i.e flowers in person's hand, or mug on the table, car on the background. Another function of this color is highlighting of text on pure white background, where yellow have no enough contrast.

However, this color is optional and can be omitted in most of the cases. Explore the usage of the style section to got an idea of how we use our palette in real-life scenarios.
