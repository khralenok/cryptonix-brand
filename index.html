Logo and mark

Our logo is word CRYPTONIX with all capital letters typed in custom font.

In all cases where full logo fits and reads well, it's usage is preferable. To define if visual have enough space to place logo take in account safe area that eqal to the 1/2 of logo letter's height. Examples of placements: website, social media graphics, documents and advertisement

Mark should be used and in the rest of the cases. For Example: avatars, favicons, pins

We also use mark as a shape or pattern. You could learn more about it in shapes and Pattern sections of this guideline
